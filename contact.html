What We Do

Here at Life's a Picnic, we strive to plan fun and unique picnics for you and your loved ones, so that you don't have to! All you need to do is tell us what you want to eat, where you want to go, and what you want to do! Then, just show up with your friends or family and be ready to have a good time!

Before you schedule a picnic with us, make sure you to check the weather! We reserve the right to cancel your picnic 12 hours in advance if weather conditions are unsafe.
